## Project Overview

This project consisted of the development of a **University Shift and Schedule Management System**, designed in the scope of the *Software Systems Development* course.  
The system supports two main roles: **Student** and **Course Director**, allowing authentication, schedule consultation, and management of students, curricular units, shifts, policies, and schedules.  

Custom functionalities include:  

- Authentication for students and course directors  
- Viewing and managing students, curricular units, and shifts  
- Generating schedules automatically or manually  
- Viewing incomplete schedules and resolving them  
- Publishing schedules and managing policies  

### Main Features:
- Modular architecture supporting students and course directors  
- Management of student registrations, curricular units, and shift assignments  
- Automatic and manual schedule generation with compatibility checks  
- Viewing schedules for students and curricular units  
- Handling incomplete schedules and alert messages for invalid operations  
- Interactive interface with options for adding/removing data and generating schedules  
- Database population via file imports (e.g., students, curricular units)  
- Error handling and informative messages
